OpenWings Wellness

OpenWings Wellness provides Counseling, Therapy, and Wellness services to individuals, couples, and families. We are dedicated to providing a safe, supportive, and non-judgmental environment for our clients to explore their thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. Our goal is to help our clients develop the skills and tools they need to live a more fulfilling and balanced life.

Services

OpenWings Wellness offers a variety of services to meet the needs of our clients. Our services include individual counseling, couples counseling, family therapy, group therapy, and wellness workshops. We also offer a range of wellness services, including yoga, meditation, and mindfulness training.
